Deep Desk Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Deep Desk ("we", "our", or "the application") is a native desktop AI assistant built with Tauri. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Storage

Deep Desk is a local-first application. Your chat history, preferences, and configuration files are stored entirely on your device. We do not operate a backend service that collects or stores your data.

2. API Keys

To connect to AI service providers, you may configure your own API keys. These keys are stored locally on your device (in ~/.deepdesk.env) and are never transmitted to us. We do not have access to your API keys.

3. AI Service Providers

When you send a message, the application forwards your request and conversation context to the AI service provider you have configured. Each provider has its own privacy policy:

  • DeepSeek — api-docs.deepseek.com
  • Anthropic — anthropic.com/privacy
  • OpenAI — openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

4. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or have access to:

  • Your chat conversations or files
  • Your API keys or credentials
  • Your IP address or device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or telemetry

5. Network Connections

The application runs a local server on localhost:3456 for communication between the desktop shell and the AI engine. This connection never leaves your device.

6. Automatic Updates

The application may check for software updates. Updates are fetched from Alibaba Cloud OSS. No personal data is included in update checks.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out at support@shieldyh.com.
